STHITI AI

A digital lifeline connecting citizens, AI intelligence, and NGOs to deliver real-time humanitarian rescue for people in distress.

Role: Product Designer + Full Stack Developer Duration: 12-Hour Hackathon • 8 Hours Development Node.js • Gemini API • SQLite • Leaflet.js

The Problem

Thousands of vulnerable people experience hunger, medical emergencies, and abandonment every day in urban areas. Citizens often want to help, but reporting systems are complicated and NGOs lack a real-time map of urgent need.

My Solution

I designed a full-stack emergency reporting ecosystem where citizens can capture a single photo, instantly share their location, and allow AI to classify emergency severity before routing the request to NGOs for action.

Key Features

One-Tap Emergency Reporting
AI Image Classification
Urgency Score Detection
NGO Command Dashboard
GPS-Based Incident Mapping
Live Status Tracking

Multi-Role Authentication System

The platform begins with a dual-entry system designed for two distinct users. Citizens can instantly report emergencies while NGO organizations access a separate command center to coordinate humanitarian assistance.

Instant Citizen Reporting

To reduce reporting friction, users simply capture a photo and submit the emergency. The browser automatically detects location coordinates and transfers them into the rescue pipeline.

Successful Incident Capture

Once the distress report is submitted, the platform confirms successful capture and pushes the image into the AI analysis workflow for immediate emergency classification.

Citizen Impact Tracking

Users can monitor their contribution and understand how their actions directly create social impact, making the platform feel transparent and encouraging repeated participation.

NGO Command Center

Organizations receive real-time rescue requests through a centralized dashboard showing emergency priority, available resources, and dispatch coordination for faster action.

Live Humanitarian Monitoring

The system continuously tracks active rescue requests, showing progress updates, urgency scores, and status transitions so organizations maintain full operational visibility.
